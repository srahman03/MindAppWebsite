our services

Track

Tracking your emotions is a systematic, continuous approach to understanding your emotional well-being. This structured process involves regular, consistent recording of emotions, often daily or in response to specific events. Using tools like apps or diaries, you gather data for in-depth analysis, gaining insights into long-term well-being. It helps identify patterns, triggers, and changes in how you feel, fostering self-awareness and personal growth over time.

Log

Logging your emotions involves capturing and recording immediate feelings in response to specific events, offering insight into reactions to various situations. Whether you're noting happiness, sadness, anger, or other emotions, you can choose to add context or triggers. This practice can be spontaneous or structured, serving as a valuable tool for self-reflection, journaling, or real-time emotional exploration.
